## Shared Samburu Context

Both properties are in Samburu Game Reserve — a northern Kenya ecosystem distinct from the Mara, Tsavo, and Amboseli. Samburu is drier, wilder in character, and home to species not found in the south: Grevy's zebra, reticulated giraffe, Beisa oryx, and gerenuk. These are called the "Samburu Special Five" (or Northern Specials).

The Samburu people are distinct from the Maasai — a common conflation to avoid.

**Shared reference points:**
- Samburu Game Reserve — northern Kenya ecosystem
- The Ewaso Ng'iro River — the lifeline of Samburu, running through the reserve
- "Northern Specials" — the five species unique to northern Kenya
- The Samburu people — distinct culture, separate from the Maasai
