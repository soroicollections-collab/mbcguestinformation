## Shared Lumo / Tsavo Context

All three properties sit within the Lumo Community Wildlife Sanctuary on the edge of Tsavo. The Sanctuary is community-managed, which is a genuine and important brand value — not a marketing add-on.

**Shared reference points:**
- Lumo Community Wildlife Sanctuary — community-managed, genuine conservation credential
- Edge of Tsavo National Park — access to one of Kenya's largest protected areas
- Taita Hills — the dramatic hill range visible from the area
- Rail safari access — all three properties are accessible via the SGR (Standard Gauge Railway) to Voi, then road transfer. This is a genuine differentiator for Nairobi travellers.
- The three properties are stacked by tier: Lions Bluff (luxury) → Leopards Lair (intimate/mid) → Cheetah Tented Camp (budget-friendly)
