Welcome

Welcome to Soroi Amboseli

We are delighted to welcome you to Soroi Amboseli — a place where Kilimanjaro rises above the treeline and the rhythm of the bush sets the pace.

Nestled in a rare forested corner of Amboseli National Park, the camp sits among the trees that make this setting unlike any other in the ecosystem. The mountain has watched over this land for millennia. We hope your time here feels unhurried.

Your camp team is here throughout your stay. Please ask us anything.

Game Drives

Into the Park

[ Open 4x4 in Amboseli with elephants and Kilimanjaro ]

Amboseli National Park is home to some of Africa's largest elephant herds, and the mountain that watches over them — Kilimanjaro, the highest peak on the continent — is visible from camp on clear days.

Game drives depart in open 4x4 safari vehicles with accredited, English-speaking guides. The early morning drive catches the elephants at the marsh as the light lifts over the mountain. The evening drive chases the light as it drops.

Schedules
  • Morning game driveDepart 06:00, return approx. 09:30
  • Afternoon / evening game driveDepart 16:00, return approx. 19:00
  • Extended drives & picnic lunchesAvailable on request

What to Bring

Neutral-coloured clothing, sunscreen, hat, binoculars, and your camera. The guides will handle everything else.

Park Fees

Amboseli National Park fees are not included in your accommodation rate and are payable separately. Current fees are charged per adult per entry. Our team can advise you on the current fee structure.

Important

Children under 8 years of age are not permitted in the Oldonyo Deluxe Suite night activities. Please speak to reception regarding any specific requirements for younger children.

Explore

Beyond the Camp

Maasai Village Visit

The Maasai have shared this landscape with the wildlife for generations. A guided visit to a local Maasai village offers an introduction to their way of life — architecture, livestock, music, and community. Speak to your guide to arrange.

Guided Bush Walk

Explore the forested setting around camp on foot with one of our guides. A different pace, a closer look.

Sundowner Experience

As the light shifts to gold, join your guide for a sundowner in the bush. Included in the Ground Package; available as an add-on for Full Board guests.

Birding

Amboseli supports over 600 bird species. Your guide can tailor a morning drive or walk around your birding interests.

Cultural & Conservation Notes

Soroi Amboseli operates within Amboseli National Park under strict wildlife conservation guidelines. Please follow your guide's instructions at all times and stay within designated areas.

Camp Services

Fire Safety

In the event of a fire, alert camp staff immediately. Assembly point: the main dining area. Do not use water on electrical fires. Fire extinguishers are located at the entrance to each tent row and at all public areas.

Wildlife Safety

Wildlife moves freely around and through the camp. Please do not walk between your tent and the main areas after dark without a camp escort. Your night runner is available on request — please call reception.
