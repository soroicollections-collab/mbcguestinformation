Welcome

Welcome to Soroi Mara Bush Camp

Dear Intrepid Traveller, Welcome, Karibu, & Sopa Oleng! Your decision to choose our small, private, and personalized camp for your stay in the world famous Maasai Mara is certainly the right one.

Get away from the stress of everyday life and enjoy the wonderful atmosphere that our special camp offers. Our idea was to build a lovely little camp where our guests could have a close encounter with nature, yet not miss out on some luxuries!

The sounds of the African Bush as well as the fascinating wildlife which surrounds the camp will make your stay with us an unforgettable experience. We're proud to hold a Gold Eco-rating level from Eco-tourism Kenya, recognizing camps that meet rigorous standards in waste, water, and energy management.

Our team is happy to welcome you and we will do our best to make this an unforgettable stay for you. Please feel free to contact any of our very competent staff at anytime and they will gladly provide any assistance you may need.

Turn-down Service

We provide turn-down services and will gladly adjust your tent's outside flaps to your requested level. Hot water bottles are provided at night for your comfort.

Wake-up Call & Morning Beverages

Our Room steward or the Camp Manager will inquire about your preferred wake-up call time each evening. You can also request early morning coffee, tea, or hot chocolate, which will be served on your tent veranda at your specified time.

Check-in / Check-out

You may occupy your tent from noon onwards. We kindly request that you check out by 10:00 am. If you require a late check-out, please ask the Manager for availability. If available, we can provide you with a day room to freshen up before departure.

Towels & Laundry

To indicate that you want your towels washed, simply place them on the floor. If you prefer to reuse them, kindly hang them on the rack to help us conserve water. Our laundry service is available as per the laundry list — items are hand washed and returned within 24 hours, weather permitting.

Game Drives

The Game Drive Experience

Feel connected to nature as you explore the wild Maasai Mara from a raised position in our custom-built, 4x4 safari vehicles. Our experienced guides will lead you to some of the most breathtaking views of the area, where you'll experience the magic of the safari — spotting elephants, lions, and cheetahs, among other fascinating wildlife.

Schedules
  • Morning game drive06:30 – 09:30+
  • Afternoon game drive15:30 – 18:30
  • Picnic breakfast on requestAsk your guide

Important Information

The vehicle may be shared with other guests. Should you require exclusive use of the vehicle, kindly book in advance with Management at an extra charge. We ensure that we adhere to wildlife/conservation etiquette during these game drives. Each drive typically lasts up to two hours, with best sightings usually occurring in early mornings or late afternoons.

What to Bring

Neutral-coloured clothing, sunscreen, hat, binoculars, and your camera. Bring your rechargeable torch to dinner if you'd like it. All other needs will be handled by your experienced guide.

Services

Camp Services

Environmental Responsibility

We take pride in our eco-friendly camps and our dedication to environmental responsibility. We are Gold Eco-rated by Eco-tourism Kenya. At Soroi Mara Bush Camp, we separate waste at source to minimize our impact. We draw water from our own borehole and heat it using eco-friendly heat pumps. Our camp operates entirely on solar power. As our guest, you will receive a complimentary eco steel bottle (on game package only) to use during game drives, promoting the reduction of single-use plastic.

Drinking Water

For your convenience, additional drinking water is available in the mini fridge. We provide a beautiful repurposed glass bottle on your bathroom sink table as your drinking water source. Our water is meticulously filtered through Reverse Osmosis to ensure the highest standards of purification and safety. If you prefer sealed bottles, they are available for purchase at the bar.

Flush Toilets & Water Saving

We kindly request to use the dual flush toilets consciously — smaller lever to flush liquid waste, and larger lever for solid waste. Please dispose of sanitary towels in the provided brown paper bags and refrain from flushing them down the toilet. We kindly ask you to use towels sparingly, to minimise water usage.

Lighting in the Tent

All lights in your tent are powered by our solar panels. To conserve energy, please switch them off when leaving your tent. The lights on the veranda can remain on for your convenience — our Maasai security / Askaris can help with this.

Internet & Connectivity

Complimentary Wi-Fi is available in all public areas and in our tents. While the signal may occasionally fluctuate or experience brief interruptions, we kindly request your patience during such instances.

Shop & Souvenirs

We welcome you to check our wonderful Boutique shop inside our photo lounge. We stock locally crafted items out of soap stone, Maasai beads & all kinds of clothing such as fleeces, branded t-shirts, bathrobes, slippers etc. We also have a COMMUNITY SHELF where we display items made by various local communities here in Kenya.

Quick Reference

Safety & Surroundings

As our camps are not fenced, we emphasise the importance of vigilance. We kindly request that you do not wander alone outside the camp, under any circumstances, as wild animals roam freely. To ensure your safety, Maasai watchmen guard the camp 24/7. During the daytime, it is safe to move around the camp independently. At night, our askari (watchman) will escort you to and from your tent.

Fire Safety

In case of any fire: Exit the tent immediately and move to a clear area. Do not stop to pick up personal belongings or attempt to salvage anything. Report any fire to a staff member immediately. Proceed to the assembly point at camp entrance for a head count. Do not re-enter until it has been declared safe by management.

Location

Situated in the heart of the Mara Game Reserve, on the Olare Orok River, we are conveniently located only 5 to 8 minutes' drive from Olkiombo Airstrip. GPS: [to be confirmed]

Social Responsibility

Through Community & Wildlife Conservation (CWC), our NGO supported by Soroi Collection, we partner with the local Maasai community to support education, sustainable livelihoods, cultural preservation, and wildlife conservation. By creating employment opportunities, investing in community projects, and protecting the natural environment, we aim to ensure tourism delivers meaningful benefits for both people and wildlife. Bed night fees from our camp are paid directly to the community, alongside monthly county fees that contribute to local development.

Valuables

For added security, a Safe is available for depositing your valuables. The camp will not be liable for any missing items, should you not utilize the safe.
