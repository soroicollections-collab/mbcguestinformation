Soroi Collection is delighted to announce the opening of our beautiful Soroi Samburu Lodge.

We will officially start welcoming guests at the lodge from the 25th of May 2026. Soroi Samburu Lodge, a stunning new addition to the Soroi Collection where luxury, wilderness, and authentic Samburu charm come together in perfect harmony.

As we near the completion of Phase Two construction of the lodge, we would like to assure all our guests and partners that the final finishing touches have been carefully planned to ensure that there will be absolutely no disruption to guest comfort and overall guest experience. Every effort has been made to ensure that we preserve the peaceful atmosphere, exceptional service, and immersive safari experience that define a stay at Soroi Samburu Lodge.

Discover the epitome wilderness adventure paired with comfort at the Soroi Samburu Lodge, this extraordinary lodge is nestled in the heart of the rugged landscapes of Samburu, offering a blend of authentic safari experiences and modern conveniences. The lodge caters to diverse preferences with a total of 49 rooms (comprising of standard, superior rooms and family units) making it an ideal choice for corporate, family getaways, conferences and incentives.
